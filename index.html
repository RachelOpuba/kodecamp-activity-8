VINSIGHTE: GIVING VISION, CREATING INSIGHT

USING TECHNOLOGY TO HELP THE VIRTUALLY INPAIRED AND BLIND

OUR GUIDING AIDS- VIRI

Aids the visually impaired in navigating their environment independently by sensing obstacles obstructions ahead and alerting the visually impaired user.

OUR SMART READING GLASSES- VISIS

Aids the visually impaired in reading books conveniently by converting texts to audio input for the visually impaired user to listen to.

OUR MOBILE APP- DR.VEE

Smart app which diagnoses eye diseases, recommends first aid treatment and refers patient to the doctor.
