Impact Stories

At Vinsighte, our aim is to reach out to about 10,000 people in the next 10 years by giving them access to our diagnostic tools, ultrasonic guiding aids, smart glasses and other services we believe would aid the visually impaired population. We hope to create an African where the visually impaired read books conveniently and move around their environments independently and confidently.

Visit to Omoyeni School

We visited Omoyeni school of the blind, Orita-Aperin, Ibadan, Oyo state where we reached out to about 45 visually impaired children. We are currently producing our products for the lovely visually impaired children in this school. Our Joy is to see them live comfortable lives, enjoy education and achieve their dreams.
